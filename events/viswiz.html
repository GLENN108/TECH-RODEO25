Event Details

VisWiz

VisWiz is a three-round data visualization competition for teams of two, with all communication strictly in English. The event begins with a 10-minute elimination MCQ round testing analytical and visual skills. Qualified teams enter a fast-paced clue-and-guess round, where technical communication is key. Finalists then work with a dataset to build a clear narrative from the insights. VisWiz highlights teamwork, clarity, and the art of telling stories through data.

Event Rules

Timing: 10:00 A.M - 12:00 P.M

  1. Two participants per team.
  2. The event comprises three rounds: Preliminary, Intermediate and Final.
  3. The communication must take place only in English.

Round 1: Preliminary (10 minutes)

  • This is an ELIMINATION round.
  • Objective-type round with visualization-based questions to test analytical and conceptual skills.

Round 2: Intermediate (1 minute per team)

  • POINTS from this round will be added to the team's score.
  • One player (Clue-Giver) faces the screen and gives only technical clues for the chart/design principle shown.
  • The other player (Guesser) faces away and must identify the concept within the time limit.

Round 3: Data Visualization (Final Round)

  • POINTS from this round will be added to the team's score.
  • Participants will be provided with a dataset and must construct a single narrative from the facts, drawing their own conclusions.
