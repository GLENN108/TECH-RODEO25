Pitch Storm

Pitch Storm is a fast-paced pitching competition where participants present their innovative ideas to a panel of judges. Showcase your communication skills, creativity, and ability to sell your concept in a limited time frame. Perfect for aspiring entrepreneurs!

PitchStorm - Shark Tank Rules

Timing: 10:45 A.M - 12:30 P.M

  1. Two participants per team.
  2. Teams must present an original business idea (product-based or service-based) strictly related to the tech field.
  3. Each team will get a 6-minute presentation followed by a 2-minute Q&A session. Presentation time limits will be strictly followed.
  4. Teams must prepare a PPT for showcasing their business idea and bring their own laptops for the presentation.
  5. Evaluation Criteria:
    • Innovative Idea
    • Feasibility & Market Potential
    • Presentation Quality
    • Clarity in Explanation
  6. General Rules:
    • No illegal or unethical concepts allowed.
    • Professional conduct is mandatory.
    • Judges' decisions are final.
    • Participants must register before the deadline.
