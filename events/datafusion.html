Data Fusion

Data Fusion – Connections is a fast-paced puzzle showdown where tech knowledge meets sharp intuition. Teams decode images, fragments, and clues to uncover hidden tech terms across escalating rounds. From simple matches to intense multi-layered connections, every second tests your clarity and teamwork. Only those who can think visually, link concepts instantly, and crack complex chains will rise to the top. Connect the clues, unlock the logic, and dominate the data maze!

DataFusion - Connections Rules

Timing: 10:00 A.M - 12:00 P.M

  1. Each team must consist of two participants.

Round 1: Simple Tech Connections

  • This is an elimination round.
  • Objective: Identify a tech term by connecting 2–3 images or words.
  • Teams must combine the clues to form a meaningful tech concept.

Round 2: Immediate Connections

  • Objective: Identify a tech word split into 2–4 fragments (images, letters, syllables).
  • Combine all fragments to form the full word.
  • Points will be added to the team's score.

Round 3: Complex Connections

  • Objective: Identify all 4 related tech/data terms from clues (images, words, diagrams).
  • Teams must write all 4 terms correctly within the time limit.
  • Points will be added to the team's score.
