ProFusion

ProFusion is a professional development event that combines technical skills with business acumen. Participate in workshops, networking sessions, and competitions designed to enhance your professional profile and career prospects.

Profusion - Project Presentation Rules

Timing: 01:30 P.M - 02:30 P.M

  1. Maximum two participants per team.
  2. Maximum ONE team per college per shift.
  3. Project Title can be chosen on your own, related to Data Analysis, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, NLP, Statistics.
  4. Mail the abstract of your project to 25pds010@loyolacollege.edu with Subject Line in this format: PP/College_Name/Dept_Name/Project_Name.
    Example: PP/Loyola_College/DataScience/Sample
  5. Last date for submission is 2nd Dec 2025, 9pm.
  6. Only shortlisted abstracts will be permitted for presentation.
  7. Each team has 8 mins (1 min for Technical Preparation, 6 mins for Presentation, 1 min for Q&A).
  8. Presentation time will be strictly followed. A buzzer will be played 1 minute before the 6 minutes allotted for presentation.
  9. Projects will be evaluated on the basis of Technical Depth, Presentation Clarity, Practical Application and Q&A.
  10. Each team must bring their own laptops for their presentation.
