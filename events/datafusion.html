Event Details

Data Fusion

Data Fusion is a competition that challenges participants to merge and analyze data from multiple sources. Demonstrate your expertise in data integration, cleaning, and transformation to create meaningful insights from complex datasets.

DataFusion – Connections Rules

Timing: 10:00 A.M - 12:00 P.M

  1. Two participants per team.
  2. The event comprises three rounds: Data Recall, Tech Connections, and Concept Express.
  3. Data Recall (Preliminary Round):
    20 tech and data science images will be displayed for 1 minute. Participants must recall and write them within 1-2 minutes.
  4. Tech Connections (Intermediate Round):
    Identify the common link between images or clues related to data science concepts.
  5. Concept Express (Final Round):
    One participant acts out a data science or tech concept while the other guesses it.
  6. Each correct answer earns 1 point; no negative points for wrong answers.
  7. Usage of smartphones, smartwatches, and Bluetooth devices is strictly prohibited.
  8. In case of a tie, a tie-breaker question will decide the winner.
