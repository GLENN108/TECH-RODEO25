TECH RODEO 25 - GENERAL RULES

  1. Registration is free and open only to students from Computer Science, Computer Applications, Data Science, IT, Mathematics, Statistics and departments related to the aforementioned.
  2. Only one team from a department can participate in each event. Participants may take part in up to two events, provided there are no timing conflicts.
  3. It is mandatory that all participants bring their College ID and Bonafide Certificate from their HoD/Principal.
  4. Online Registration closes on 03-12-2025 at 04:00 P.M. ON-SPOT Registration is from 07:00 A.M to 08:30 A.M at MCA BLOCK Ground Floor.
  5. All participants, even those registered online, must report to the On-Spot Registration Desk to collect their participation card. Failure to do so will result in cancellation of registration.
  6. No participants will be allowed inside the college after 08:15 A.M as the college gates will be closed.
  7. Participants who leave the college premises will not be allowed inside again.
  8. Participants must make their own arrangements for Lunch and Refreshments. The College Canteen will be open on the day.
  9. Please do not bring any food items into the lab or classroom. We cannot be held responsible for any valuable items.
  10. Dress Code:
    • a. Boys – Formal Shirts/Collared T-Shirts, Full Pants (Round Neck T-Shirts, Torn Jeans, Hoodies are not allowed).
    • b. Girls – Formal Dress (Short Tops, Torn Jeans, Round Neck T-Shirts are not allowed).
  11. Non-compliance with the time limit, rules, and code of conduct will result in immediate disqualification.
  12. Decisions of the Judges are final and binding.
  13. Announcement of Results and Prize Distribution will be held only during the Valedictory Function (03:00 P.M).
