Event Details

Pitch Storm

Pitch Storm is a a battle of brilliance where innovation meets persuasion. Teams step into the spotlight to sell their most groundbreaking tech ideas with a sharp, high-impact pitch. With only minutes to impress, clarity, originality, and market vision become the ultimate weapons. Whether through on-spot case cracking or a direct pitch faceoff, only the most compelling ideas will survive the storm. Convince the judges, outshine the competition, and turn your concept into the next big tech breakthrough!

PitchStorm - Shark Tank Rules

Timing: 2:00 P.M - 3:30 P.M

  1. Each team must have two participants and present an original tech-based business idea using a PPT.
  2. Each team will be given 6 minutes for presentation and 2 minutes for Q&A with strict time control.
  3. All ideas must be legal, ethical, and presented professionally, with mandatory registration before the deadline.
  4. Evaluation will focus on innovation, feasibility, market potential, presentation quality, and clarity.
  5. If participants are many, the event will have two rounds: an on-spot case study prelims followed by a final PPT pitch for selected teams (6-minute presentation + 2-minute Q&A).
  6. If participants are fewer, the event will be held as a single direct PPT pitching round with the same timing.
  7. Teams must bring their own laptops for presenting their PPT.
  8. In case of a tie, clarity of explanation, uniqueness of the idea, and practical feasibility will determine the winner.
