ProFusion

ProFusion - Project Presentation is your stage to showcase innovation, clarity, and real-world impact through cutting-edge data projects. Teams distill their ideas into a powerful, time-bound pitch that highlights technical depth and practical relevance. With crisp presentations, smart demos, and sharp Q&A, every second becomes a chance to impress the judges. From ML models to dashboards and NLP solutions, only the most compelling and well-crafted projects will rise above the rest. Step in, present with confidence, and let your ideas speak louder than words!

Profusion - Project Presentation Rules

Timing: 01:30 P.M - 02:30 P.M

  1. Maximum two participants per team.
  2. Project Title can be chosen on your own, related to Data Analysis, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, NLP, Statistics.
  3. Each team has 5 mins (1 min for Technical Preparation, 3 mins for Presentation, 1 min for Q&A).
  4. Presentation time will be strictly followed. A buzzer will be played 1 minute before the 4 minutes allotted for presentation.
  5. Projects will be evaluated on the basis of Technical Depth, Presentation Clarity, Practical Application and Q&A.
  6. Each team must bring their own laptops for their presentation. Presentation slides must be in PPT/PDF format. Use college-appropriate language, visuals, and citations.
  7. If the project includes a model, dashboard, or application, it is encouraged. But no internet access will be provided.
  8. Participants must report 10–15 minutes before their scheduled presentation. Delayed teams may lose their slot.
  9. The judges' decision will be final and binding. No appeals will be entertained.
