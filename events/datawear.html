Event Details

DataWear

DataWear is a unique event focused on wearable technology and data integration. Explore the intersection of fashion, technology, and data science by creating innovative wearable devices that collect and analyze real-time data.

Data Wear – T-Shirt Painting Rules

Timing: 01:30 P.M - 02:30 P.M

  1. Two participants per team.
  2. The artwork must be relevant to the theme, which will be announced on the spot.
  3. Participants must bring their own materials, including a plain white T-shirt (size L), fabric or acrylic paints, glitters, brushes, a mixing plate, pencil, pen, and eraser.
  4. Usage of any pictorial reference, ready-made stickers and other materials is strictly prohibited.
  5. Tracing or copying in any form is not allowed.
  6. Evaluation Criteria:
    • Creativity
    • Visual Impact
    • Quality of Application
    • Effectiveness in Expressing the Theme
  7. Usage of mobile phones, smartwatches, and bluetooth devices is strictly prohibited during the competition.
