Event Details

Rodeo Hunt

Rodeo Hunt - Treasure Hunt is a fast-paced chase for clues where logic, speed, and teamwork decide your path to victory. Teams crack puzzles, crosswords, and tricky aptitude questions before diving into a high-stakes final round of math, stats, and CS challenges. With every clue solved, you move closer to the hidden finish—but one wrong step can cost precious time. No gadgets, no shortcuts—only pure brainpower and collaboration. Saddle up, solve smart, and hunt your way to the treasure!

Rodeo Hunt - Treasure Hunt Rules

Timing: 01:30 P.M - 02:30 P.M

  1. Two participants per team.
  2. The event comprises two rounds: Preliminary and Final.

Preliminary Round (25 min):

  • Questions will be based on general aptitude, including puzzles, crosswords, and similar challenges.

Final Round (25 min):

  • Questions will cover basic Mathematics, Statistics, and Computer Science (Python, Java), with an emphasis on problem-solving.
  1. Usage of mobile phones, smartwatches, Bluetooth devices, and internet access is strictly prohibited during the competition and will lead to immediate disqualification.
